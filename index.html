    ABOUT TYRE EXPRESS

    Tyre Express is a subsidiary of Tay & Becca Services. At Tyre Express, we are your ultimate destination for high-quality tires available for purchase. We take pride in offering a wide range of tire sizes and brands to customers across Nigeria and select parts of Africa, including Chad, Benin Republic, and more.

    Our mission is to provide you with convenient access to premium tires that meet your specific needs. Whether you're searching for tires that offer superior traction, enhanced durability, or fuel efficiency, we have you covered. With Tyre Express, you can trust us to deliver the perfect tires for your vehicle, while ensuring a seamless purchasing experience.

    Our tires have successfully obtained various certifications, including China CCC, US DOT, EU E-mark, EU Regulation 117, Gulf Country GCC, Saudi Arabia SASO, and Nigeria SONCAP.
